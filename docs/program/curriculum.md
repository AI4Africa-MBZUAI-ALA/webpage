# Curriculum

The curriculum centers on how young leaders can understand, evaluate, and use AI responsibly in real community contexts.

## Themes

- AI basics and terminology
- Prompting and practical AI workflows
- Data, bias, and fairness
- Responsible AI and governance
- Leadership for community problem-solving
- Team collaboration and presentation skills

## Design principles

- Keep examples relevant to African contexts
- Prefer practical exercises over abstract theory
- Connect each topic to a real-world use case
- Make room for discussion, reflection, and iteration
