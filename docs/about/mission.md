# Mission

The mission of the AI for Africa Bootcamp 2026 is to empower future African leaders to use AI responsibly to solve meaningful community challenges.

## What that means in practice

- Build confidence with core AI concepts
- Connect technical learning to local community needs
- Practice responsible and ethical decision-making
- Strengthen leadership, collaboration, and communication

## Why this matters

Africa's future leaders need both practical AI literacy and a strong sense of responsibility. This bootcamp is designed to help participants think critically about how AI can support communities, institutions, and public-good projects.
