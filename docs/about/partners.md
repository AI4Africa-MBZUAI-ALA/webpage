# Partners

The bootcamp is a collaboration between:

- African Leadership Academy (ALA)
- Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI)

## Partnership focus

The partnership combines leadership development, educational experience, and AI expertise to create a residential bootcamp experience for students from across Africa.

## Working model

- ALA supports leadership and student development context
- MBZUAI contributes AI expertise and academic perspective
- Program organizers coordinate the residential experience in Johannesburg
