# FAQ

## What is the AI for Africa Bootcamp 2026?

It is a two-week residential AI and leadership bootcamp in Johannesburg for approximately 40 students from across Africa.

## Who is organizing it?

The bootcamp is a collaboration between African Leadership Academy (ALA) and Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI).

## What is the goal of the program?

The goal is to empower future African leaders to use AI responsibly to solve meaningful community challenges.

## Who updates the website?

Mentors and organizers can update the Markdown pages directly so the site stays easy to maintain.

## Is the site mobile friendly?

Yes. The site is built with MkDocs Material, which provides a responsive layout and a built-in search experience.
