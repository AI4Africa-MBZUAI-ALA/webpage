# AI for Africa Bootcamp 2026

The AI for Africa Bootcamp 2026 is a two-week residential AI and leadership program in Johannesburg, created through a collaboration between African Leadership Academy (ALA) and Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI).

The bootcamp brings together approximately 40 students from across Africa to explore how AI can be used responsibly to solve meaningful community challenges.
