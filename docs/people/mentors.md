# Mentors

Mentors help participants connect ideas, shape projects, and stay focused on responsible problem-solving.

## Mentor responsibilities

- Support group discussions and project framing
- Encourage responsible and ethical thinking
- Help teams clarify a community challenge
- Provide feedback on presentations and prototypes

## How to use this site

Mentors should be able to update guidance, session notes, and program updates directly in Markdown. Keep this page as the place for mentoring guidance and expectations.
